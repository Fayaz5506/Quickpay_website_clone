Available Services

Quickpay provides several services to help you integrate and manage payments in your application.

Payment Services

  • Credit Card Processing
  • MobilePay Integration

  • Google Pay Integration
  • Visa/Mastercard Processing

Subscription Services

Manage recurring payments and subscriptions with our comprehensive subscription service.

  • Recurring Billing
  • Subscription Management
  • Payment Schedule Control
  • Customer Management

Payout Services

Handle payouts and transfers with our secure payout service.

  • Automated Payouts
  • Batch Processing
  • Payout Scheduling
  • Transaction History

  • API Integration - Full control over the payment flow
